Our Namesake

OSR is an acronym, and as much as much as I would like it to also stand for some cool development terminology, it doesn’t. OSR is a place and more specifically, it’s a road.
Texas history digression: This road was used as early as 1691 as a major thorough fare from Mexico to East Texas. Feel free to Google on your own time, but it played a crucial role in Texas and American history.
Arguably, more importantly, it played a huge role in my own personal history. Along this really important road, there is a small ranch {Any visions about a really cool Texas ranch with lots of horses to ride and rivers to swim in - toss them. Picture more flat never-ending pastures, a lot of cows and some chickens} My grandparents purchased this ranch when I was young and around the age of 7, convinced my siblings and I to work on said ranch. Every summer through high school, we worked from 6am-3pm doing extremely thrilling chores like painting miles of fence, mowing fields, fixing the fences, feeding cows, picking vegetables and cleaning out barns.
{I assure you that the point is coming}
All of those monotonous chores that seemed to have no purpose but to torture me, taught me probably the most valuable lesson that I could have ever learned: All of the hard work that you put into the seemingly small details will manifest in the end. There is no greater satisfaction than to see the work that we did 15+ years ago holding up against the elements and time. We worked hard to do our jobs right the first time and we made a pretty awesome business and family retreat.
Choosing the name OSR was meant to signify the hard work and dedication that we’ll put into your project. Nothing that represents your company is too insignificant of a task to devote our full attention and care to detail.

Renee Dudley,
Founder

Our Process From Your Vision


  • 1. In Vino Veritas

    The Romans were on to something when they declared that "in wine, truth". The first step of this journey is to meet and talk about your baby, your company. Your website should be an expression of your core values and what you envision for the future of your company. At this meeting we'll talk about the type of clients you want to attract, what you imagine your website to be, any and all expectations, and most importantly we'll set up a communication plan.

  • 2. Feild trip

    This is the fun part for us! We want to experience the product or service that you offer. In order to accuratley represent you and your company online, we think it's essential to dive into the customer experience first hand. This stage will also include user and competitor research. You know what they say, keep your friends close and your enimes closer...

  • 3. Inspire Us

    We want to create a site that conveys the values and style that you had in mind when you created your company, so we gotta get inside your head. You'll create pintrest board of any and all colors, textures, sites, fonts, pictures, cute animals, or anything else that you want expressed on your site. Of course we can't incorporate it all, the goal here is to get a firm understanding of your vision for the site. Once submitted your get 2 revisions.

  • 4. We Inspire You

    It's our turn. We take everything that you have given and shown us and we present to you a direction sheet. This sheet will have the color scheme, fonts, your logo, and any prominent images that will be featured on your site. If this website was a house, this would be where we lay the foundation. Once submitted you will get 2 revisions.

  • 5.Back to the lab

    It's time for us to get creative AF. This is the step where we get our creative design juices flowing and begin to arrange your site into a one of a kind design. This is where we build the walls and set the layout for the house (house analogy throwback).

  • 6. Develop

    This is where the magic happens. Your site will go from informative to a work of art. We'll put in functionality, style, and SEO. Time to decorate the house.

  • 7. QA and Testing

    QA is fancy computer terminology for quality assurance. Essentially we click on ALL THE THINGS and spell check everything. We make sure everything works on your site so that your customers won't have any issues when the site is launched. Once we send the final product to you, your get 3 revisions.

  • 8. Launch

    Your site will be live on the world wide web. We'll give you materials for notifying your customers of your new site and do a happy dance, because the masterpiece is complete. Once the site is live, you'll recieve one month of complimentary maintence {3 revisions}.

  • 9. Back to the bar

    Once your site goes live, we're gonna take you back to the bar to celebrate and de-brief. We'll discuss a maintence plan, give you all sorts of consults, and finally celebrate your success over a cold beer.
